❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Aapke kuchh common sawaalon ke jawaab.

ID kaise khareedein?

1. Main Menu mein 'Buy 8 Level ID' button par click karein. 2. 'Google', 'Facebook', ya 'X' mein se ek platform chunein. 3. Kitne ID chahiye, uska pack select karein. 4. Payment page par 'Proceed to Payment' karein aur payment karke screenshot bhejein.

Paid Push kaise kaam karta hai?

1. 'CS Rank Paid Push' button par click karein. 2. Apna plan chunein (jaise 10 Stars, 25 Stars, etc.). 3. Payment complete karein. 4. Approval ke baad aapko bot se confirmation milega aur aapse UID maangi jayegi. Phir aapko 'Waiting List' me daal diya jayega. Jab aapki baari aayegi, bot aapko aage ke instructions dega.

Wallet ka istemal kaise karein?

'My Wallet' button par click karein. Aap 'Add Funds' karke paise daal sakte hain (minimum ₹50) ya 'Withdraw' karke paise nikal sakte hain (minimum ₹50). Khareedari ke waqt wallet se pay karne ka option bhi aayega.

ID kab milegi?

Payment verification ke baad ID turant deliver kar di jaati hai. Agar 10 minute mein na mile toh support se contact karein.
